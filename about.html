Our Story

Founded in 2010, FAMM ATL Company Ltd began as a small agricultural trading company in Tanzania with a vision to connect Tanzanian farmers with global markets. What started as a modest operation with just three employees has grown into a leading agricultural export company with international reach.

Our name, FAMM ATL, represents our core values: Farming, Agriculture, Marketing, Management with Advanced Trading & Logistics. These principles guide every aspect of our business operations.

Over the past decade, we've built strong relationships with over 500 local farmers across Tanzania, implemented sustainable farming practices, and established reliable export channels to markets worldwide. Our commitment to quality and reliability has made us a trusted partner for international buyers seeking premium African agricultural products.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To be the bridge between Tanzanian agricultural producers and global markets by providing high-quality products, reliable export services, and creating sustainable value for farmers, customers, and communities.

To become Africa's most trusted agricultural export company, known for exceptional quality, sustainable practices, and transformative impact on farming communities.
