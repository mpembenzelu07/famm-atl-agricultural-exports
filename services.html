Our Export Process

A streamlined process ensuring efficiency and reliability from order to delivery.

1

Inquiry & Quotation

We receive your inquiry, assess requirements, and provide a detailed quotation including pricing, timeline, and terms.

2

Order Confirmation

Upon agreement, we prepare a sales contract, request deposit payment, and confirm the order.

3

Sourcing & Quality Check

We source the products, conduct quality inspections, and prepare them for export with proper packaging.

4

Documentation & Customs

We handle all export documentation, obtain necessary certificates, and clear customs.

5

Shipping & Tracking

Products are shipped via selected method with real-time tracking provided to the customer.

6

Delivery & After-sales

Products are delivered to destination and we provide after-sales support for customer satisfaction.
