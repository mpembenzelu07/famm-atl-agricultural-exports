About FAMM ATL Company Ltd

Our Story

Founded in 2010, FAMM ATL Company Ltd began as a small agricultural trading company in Tanzania. Over the years, we have grown into a leading exporter of premium agricultural products, connecting Tanzanian farmers with international markets.

Our name, FAMM ATL, represents our commitment to Farming, Agriculture, Marketing, and Management with Advanced Trading & Logistics. We take pride in supporting local farmers while delivering exceptional quality to our global customers.

Our Mission

To be the bridge between Tanzanian agricultural producers and global markets by providing high-quality products, reliable export services, and creating sustainable value for all stakeholders.

Our Values

Quality

We never compromise on the quality of our products and services.

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and fairness.

Sustainability

We promote sustainable farming practices and environmental stewardship.

Our Team

Our team consists of experienced agronomists, quality control specialists, logistics experts, and trade professionals who ensure that every shipment meets our high standards and customer expectations.

We work closely with agricultural extension officers to support our partner farmers with best practices, quality seeds, and sustainable farming techniques.
